Introduction

Hi! My name is Ioannis Paraschos. I'm currently a high school Junior attending the Academy for the Arts, Science, and Technology (also known as AAST). I've attended AAST since my ninth grade year; when I was a tenth grader, I chose to pursue the Computer Science Major offered at AAST and began taking its courses in my eleventh grade year. As I was in the school longer and longer, I began becoming more involved with AAST and the community of students around it. Currently, I'm one of the Junior Class Representatives of AAST and the President of AAST's National Beta Chapter.
